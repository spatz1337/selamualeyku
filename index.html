About Slimekeep

Slimekeep is a fast-paced rogue-like with unique decision making and intriguing player freedoms, where you kill and capture various slimes to stop the corruption that has infected the land. Venture through procedurally generated levels, and stop the slime king before it's too late.

About this game

Slimekeep is a fast paced action roguelike with a heavy emphasis on decision making and player freedom. Adventure through procedurally generated levels, in a quest to stop the spread of corruption created by the Slime King. You'll have the choice to fight or capture slimes, visit the Slime King's lair, or hold off another day, and so much more. Think Enter the Gungeon meets Slime Rancher, with additional unique mechanics, and elements.
